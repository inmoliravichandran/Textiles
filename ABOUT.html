Vision And Mission

Our Vision And Mission

We work with the vision of becoming the best garment manufacturers in the world. At Cheer Sagar Exports, we aim to achieve this vision through well-defined objectives and strategies. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of our business strategy and policy. We believe happy customers are a result of happy employees and hence we respect and value the contribution of our team in taking our business to new heights. To earn customer satisfaction by delivering nothing less than the perfect – by utilizing the best of resources and world-class manufacturing infrastructure. We are committed to the consistent upliftment of our products and our responsibility towards our employees. We work with the mission to be recognized as the leader in manufacturing, supplying, and exporting high-quality fabrics and garments across the world. We make use of the best technology to deliver quality textile products at the most competitive prices to maintain a long-term relationship with our clients.

Delivering Values

We believe in delivering the best services to our customers by utilizing high-quality raw material and resources in the manufacturing of products. Our smooth delivery process ensures that the product reaches you in the prescribed period.

Ethical Approach

We follow internationally approved business ethics that distinguish us from our competitors. Honesty, human dignity, and integrity is the base of our relationship with our clients, stakeholders, and employees.

We Are Professionals

We are aware of our responsibilities and take measures to provide the best service to our customers and meet all levels of customer satisfaction and expectation. All our services are customer-oriented.

Company Details

About Us

Established in 1996 as a partnership firm – Cheer Sagar Exports is the leading manufacturer and exporter of women apparel and home décor accessories. Our range of products includes ladies blouses, women’s dresses, Kurtas for women, skirts, handbags, quilts, curtains, mattresses, and other important clothing and home décor items.

Our exclusive range of women apparel is designed keeping in mind the needs and preferences of our invaluable clients. All products are manufactured using high-quality raw materials to design attractive, skin-friendly, and beautiful apparel that are priced for perfection. We also customize our range according to the demand of our esteemed clientele. The products are delivered via expert and high-quality delivery services.

We carry out our business operations under the guidance and prudence of our managing director – Mr. Rakshit Poddar. He brings on board years of experience in the garment industry and sharp business acumen and ethical approach. We readily accept bulk orders from our clients and ensure to fulfill them within the expected period. The key to our success lies in our highly advanced and ruggedly constructed infrastructure and expert employees. We take care of designing & production, administrative, sales & marketing, warehousing & packaging, research & development, logistics, quality control, and many other administrative departments. Our highly experienced team of skilled professionals have rich domain expertise and thus can accomplish targets in set periods.

What makes us unique?

Rich industry experience of more than 30 years. Wide and comprehensive range of women’s clothing and home décor accessories – everything under one roof. Expert team of skilled professionals who understand the value of time and quality for our clients. Ability to customize orders as per customer’s demand. Ability to take bulk orders and fulfill them within specified time period.

Global Presence

Global Presence

As the leading manufacturer of quality readymade apparels, we have worked with established brand owners, trading houses designer labels, and international store chains across the world. Under the guidance of Shri G P Poddar we established a very good relationship with the clients. The major countries we cater to are South America, Japan, Europe, etc.

The manufacturing unit is spread over 125,000 square feet, employing 1000+ skilled personnel along with a training and HR centre. Our fabric sourcing unit is set up across India and in China with their offices in Delhi, Mumbai, and Ahmedabad. Our product range includes knitted garments for ladies and kids, as well as high-quality home furnishing items including cushion covers, bedspreads, quilts, bags, accessories, footwear, etc.
